Description

The artwork explores the depth of a feeling deeply rooted in the mind, represented as a figure among crystals and diamonds.

This feeling, elusive and unattainable, has resisted every attempt to be erased or distorted.

The central figure, shrouded in shadows and fragments of color, symbolizes the complexity of an inner presence that is both fragile and immutable. The crystalline forms emerging around the figure embody the purity and strength of this feeling, which persists despite every effort to remove it.

The artwork suggests an inner struggle with an emotion that, no matter how indefinable, remains constantly present, embedded in the soul like an incorruptible crystal.
