Crossing Boundaries

My work invites you to step into this tension, challenge your perspective, and discover the hidden depths of your emotions. Each bubble and fracture is not merely a visual element but a representation of the ideas and sensations that often float in our minds, sometimes in conflict, sometimes in balance.

This visual journey is an invitation to confront your doubts, embrace uncertainty, and allow your beliefs to evolve. The bubbles floating in the air symbolize our ideas, free yet fragile, ready to burst into new understandings. In this dance of forms and shadows, the black ink represents the limits we impose on ourselves, those boundaries we draw, trapping ourselves in a web of self-imposed pressures.

Every stroke, every bubble, and every crack is a call to recognize these self-imposed constraints and to break free from them, allowing our perceptions to expand and transform.
