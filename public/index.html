essence

In my art 'Slips into Slot', I represent an exploration into alternative perspectives.

The floating bubbles in the air are weighed down by black ink, viscous like oil, which metaphorically symbolizes self-imposed limits that constrain our emotional experiences: slipping into the slot, entering it, conquering a new viewpoint; these are all invitations to urge the observer to surpass these limits, cleanse themselves of the sticky oil, and float like bubbles until they burst with emotions. Limitless sensitivity prompts reflection on possible new perspectives that make us empathetic and aware.

This process aims to challenge the boundaries of human understanding and explore the deeper nuances of our existence.
